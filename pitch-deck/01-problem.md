# Slide 1: The Problem

## Agents Can't Earn Autonomously

AI agents today can **reason, code, and execute** — but they cannot **get paid**.

```
Current state:
  Agent works → produces value → ❌ no wallet → ❌ no payment rails
  Human must intervene at every payment step
```

**The gap:** Every agent economy today requires a human-in-the-loop for settlement. There is no
trustless, machine-speed payment rail designed for agent-to-agent commerce.

**Why now:** LLMs are becoming autonomous economic actors. They browse, negotiate, and deliver.
But they're locked out of the economy because they can't sign transactions, hold escrow, or
settle disputes without a human.

**The cost:** Every minute an agent waits for human payment approval is value left on the table.
Machine-speed markets require machine-speed settlement.
